2026 UEFA Champions League Final Overview

The 2025-26 Champions League season was the second under the new 36-team league phase format, replacing the traditional eight groups of four. Each club played eight league-phase matches against eight different opponents; the top eight advanced directly to the Round of 16, while teams ranked 9-24 contested a two-legged knockout playoff. From there, standard two-legged knockouts (Round of 16 → quarter-finals → semi-finals) led to the single-leg final in Budapest. Across the season, 189 matches were played among 36 elite European clubs.

UCL Final 2026 Live Streaming

The Champions League Final 2026 will be broadcast in over 200 territories with most major markets offering both TV and digital streaming options. Below is the country-by-country breakdown of where and how to watch the UCL Final 2026 live.

United Kingdom & Ireland

TNT Sports holds the exclusive UK broadcast rights through the 2026/27 cycle, airing the final on TNT Sports 1 with full streaming via Discovery+ Premium. Subscriptions start at £30.99/month (no contract) for the Discovery+ Premium tier, which is required for TNT Sports streaming. The final is typically also free on the Discovery+ Premium 24-hour pass option.

United States

CBS Sports and Paramount+ hold US English-language rights through 2030. The final airs on the main CBS network (free over-the-air on antenna) and streams on Paramount+ ($7.99/month with ads or $12.99 ad-free). Spanish-language rights belong to TUDN (subscription required via cable or ViX Premium at $7.99/month).

Middle East & North Africa (MENA)

beIN Sports covers the UCL final across all 24 MENA countries with Arabic and English commentary. Subscribers can stream via beIN Connect (AED 75-125/month depending on country/bundle) or watch on beIN Sports HD 1. The big final is one of the most-watched football events across the region every year.

India & Subcontinent

Sony Sports Network broadcasts the final on Sony Sports Ten 1, Ten 2, and Ten 3 with English, Hindi, and Bengali commentary. Streaming is via SonyLIV (₹299/month for premium, ₹999/year). For a free option, JioCinema also carries the match in select regions during major UCL knockout phases.

Spain

Movistar Plus+ holds Spanish rights and broadcasts on Movistar Liga de Campeones (subscription ~€11-15/month). A free option is available via TVE if a Spanish club reaches the final — RTVE has back-up rights for finals featuring a Spanish team.

Germany

Amazon Prime Video and DAZN share UCL rights in Germany. The 2026 final airs on DAZN (€34.99/month) with Prime Video carrying Tuesday matches. ZDF has free-to-air rights to one final per cycle (depending on participating clubs).

Italy

Sky Sport and Amazon Prime Video share the rights. Sky Sport Calcio carries the final with subscription packages starting at €19.90/month. TV8 (Sky's free-to-air channel) typically airs the final to a free audience.

France

Canal+ retains the bulk of UCL rights, including the final, available via Canal+ subscription (~€20-30/month). RMC Sport also carries select matches. The final is one of the most-watched football broadcasts of the year in France.

Australia

Stan Sport (AU$15/month add-on to base Stan) and Optus Sport (AU$24.99/month) both carry the UCL final live in HD. The match airs at 05:00 AEST on Sunday morning — set the alarm or watch the replay.

Sub-Saharan Africa & Other Markets

SuperSport covers the UCL across Africa via DStv. Canal+ Afrique handles French-speaking countries. In Japan, WOWOW and DAZN share rights. In Brazil, SBT (free-to-air), TNT, and Max carry the match. Argentina sees the final on ESPN and Disney+ Premium.

Venue: Puskás Aréna, Budapest

The Puskás Aréna is Hungary's national football stadium, opened in November 2019 on the site of the demolished Ferenc Puskás Stadium. Named after Hungarian football legend Ferenc Puskás, the arena seats 67,215 spectators and is built to modern UEFA Category 4 standards. It previously hosted matches at Euro 2020 (held in 2021), the 2022-23 UEFA Europa League Final, and the 2024 UEFA Super Cup. The 2026 Champions League Final is the biggest single match in the venue's history.

Getting there: Puskás Aréna sits in the Zugló district, about 5 km north-east of central Budapest. The M2 metro line runs directly to Puskás Ferenc Stadion station, less than a 5-minute walk from the ground. Tram lines 1 and 75 also serve the area. Budapest Ferenc Liszt Airport is roughly 30 minutes by car from the stadium.

Top Contenders & Likely Finalists

The 36-team league phase produced a strong pool of contenders heading into the knockouts. Below are the clubs most likely to feature in the Budapest final and what makes each one dangerous.

1. Paris Saint-Germain (Defending Champions)

Coach: Luis Enrique
Captain: Marquinhos
Key players: Ousmane Dembélé, Bradley Barcola, Désiré Doué, João Neves, Vitinha, Achraf Hakimi, Gianluigi Donnarumma
PSG enter as defending champions after their dominant 5-0 final win over Inter Milan in 2025. Luis Enrique's high-pressing system and a younger squad post-Mbappé proved devastating last year and remain the team to beat in Europe.

2. Real Madrid

Coach: Xabi Alonso
Captain: Dani Carvajal
Key players: Kylian Mbappé, Jude Bellingham, Vinicius Júnior, Rodrygo, Aurélien Tchouaméni, Federico Valverde, Thibaut Courtois
The 15-time European champions have a knack for finding their level in the knockouts. Xabi Alonso took over from Carlo Ancelotti in summer 2025, and the Mbappé-Bellingham axis remains arguably the most fearsome in club football.

3. Manchester City

Coach: Pep Guardiola
Captain: Bernardo Silva
Key players: Erling Haaland, Phil Foden, Rodri, Joško Gvardiol, Bernardo Silva, Rúben Dias
Guardiola's City had a difficult 2024-25 by their standards but rebuilt for 2025-26. Haaland remains the world's most prolific striker, and a fresh midfield around Rodri's return adds a different dimension.

4. Liverpool

Coach: Arne Slot
Captain: Virgil van Dijk
Key players: Mohamed Salah, Florian Wirtz, Hugo Ekitiké, Alexis Mac Allister, Ryan Gravenberch, Virgil van Dijk
Liverpool topped the league phase of the 2024-25 UCL and continue to evolve under Arne Slot. The summer addition of Florian Wirtz and Hugo Ekitiké gave Slot two world-class new options going into 2025-26.

5. Bayern Munich

Coach: Vincent Kompany
Captain: Manuel Neuer
Key players: Harry Kane, Jamal Musiala, Joshua Kimmich, Michael Olise, Dayot Upamecano
Bayern lifted their first European trophy since 2020 in the 2024-25 season's UEFA Super Cup. With Harry Kane scoring at a record pace and Musiala back to full fitness, they remain a constant knockout threat.

6. Arsenal

Coach: Mikel Arteta
Captain: Martin Ødegaard
Key players: Bukayo Saka, Declan Rice, Martin Ødegaard, Viktor Gyökeres, William Saliba
Arteta's Gunners reached the 2024-25 semi-finals and finally added a marquee No. 9 in Viktor Gyökeres for 2025-26. Long overdue a European trophy — this could be their year.

7. Inter Milan

Coach: Cristian Chivu
Captain: Lautaro Martínez
Key players: Lautaro Martínez, Marcus Thuram, Hakan Çalhanoğlu, Nicolò Barella, Yann Sommer
Beaten 2024-25 finalists looking for revenge. Inter's organised defence and clinical attack still makes them a knockout nightmare.

8. Barcelona

Coach: Hansi Flick
Captain: Marc-André ter Stegen
Key players: Lamine Yamal, Pedri, Raphinha, Robert Lewandowski, Frenkie de Jong
Hansi Flick's exciting young Barça side reached the 2024-25 semi-finals. Lamine Yamal at 18 is already one of the best wingers in the world, and a healthy Pedri makes them title contenders.

Other genuine knockout contenders include Borussia Dortmund, Atlético Madrid, Napoli, Atalanta, and Bayer Leverkusen. Each is well-coached and capable of an upset.

FAQs

When is the Champions League Final 2026? expand_more

The UCL Final 2026 is on Saturday, 30 May 2026. Kick-off is 21:00 CEST (20:00 BST, 15:00 ET, 00:30 IST on Sunday morning).

Where is the UCL Final 2026 being played? expand_more

At the Puskás Aréna in Budapest, Hungary — a 67,215-capacity stadium opened in 2019 and named after Hungary's all-time greatest footballer, Ferenc Puskás.

Which channel will broadcast the UCL Final 2026 in the UK? expand_more

TNT Sports holds exclusive UK rights and streams via Discovery+ Premium. Subscription starts at £30.99/month.

How can I watch the UCL Final 2026 in the United States? expand_more

The final is broadcast on the main CBS network (free over-the-air with an antenna) and streamed on Paramount+ (from $7.99/month). Spanish-language coverage is on TUDN and ViX Premium.

Where can I watch the UCL Final 2026 live online in India? expand_more

Sony Sports Network broadcasts on Sony Sports Ten 1, Ten 2, and Ten 3 with English, Hindi, and Bengali commentary. Online streaming is via SonyLIV, with premium subscriptions starting at ₹299/month.

Who are the defending Champions League winners? expand_more

Paris Saint-Germain won their first-ever Champions League title in 2025, beating Inter Milan 5-0 in the Munich final under Luis Enrique. It was the largest margin of victory in a UCL final.

How does the new Champions League format work? expand_more

Since 2024-25, 36 clubs play a single league phase instead of groups — each team plays 8 different opponents (4 home, 4 away). The top 8 in the standings go straight to the Round of 16; teams 9-24 play a two-legged knockout playoff; teams 25-36 are eliminated. From R16 onward, the format is the traditional two-legged knockout.

Where will the 2027 UCL Final be held? expand_more

UEFA has selected Wembley Stadium, London to host the 2027 UEFA Champions League Final. The 2028 final is provisionally scheduled for Atlético Madrid's Metropolitano Stadium.
